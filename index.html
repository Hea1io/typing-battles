How To Play

Welcome to Typing Battles!

The Goal

Type accurately and quickly to defeat literacy bosses or improve your WPM!

How to Start

Click on the blurred text to focus it, then start typing. The text will become clear when you click it.

Correct Letters

When you type a letter correctly it turns green. This damages the boss!

Speed = Damage

The faster you type, the more damage you deal to the boss!

  • 20+ WPM: 1.5x damage
  • 40+ WPM: 2x damage
  • 60+ WPM: 3x damage

Boss Special Attack

When a boss drops below 50% HP, they unleash a special attack!

  • Shakespeare: Scrambled the remaining text, meaning you have to remeber what it was before to type it correctly.
  • Robert Frost: Not even in the game yet
  • Edgar Allan Poe: Not even in the game yet

Winning

Defeat all bosses to become the Typing Master!

Pro Tips

  • Focus on accuracy first, speed will come naturally
  • Stay calm during boss special attacks
  • Use Practice Mode to warm up before boss battles
